Resources I recommend using, for learning web development:

freeCodeCamp Codecademy W3Schools DevTips

Personally, I recommend following the 'Learn HTML & CSS: Part I'
course by Codecademy, to get the basic knowledge of HTML and CSS.
Then, I'd say watch some DevTips videos, they can be really helpful
and fun to watch. Use W3Schools, to look up HTML/CSS tags. Once you
feel confident with HTML and CSS, I'd recommend using freeCodeCamp.
I didn't feel like I was going to enjoy it at first, but after
learning on there, and chatting with the community, I'd have to say
it's a must for a free learner like me!
